# Why the old K still appears in "Open in app"

The tab favicon and the "Open in app" badge are different systems.

If Chrome shows the clock in the tab but the old K inside "Open in app", the website favicon is already fixed. The old K is stored inside a locally installed Chrome web app profile.

Fix on your machine:

1. Open Chrome.
2. Go to `chrome://apps`.
3. Right-click the KAIROS/runkairos app.
4. Remove from Chrome.
5. Reload `https://runkairos.com/?enterprise=20260702`.

Alternative:
- Click the app/open icon in the address bar.
- Open app settings.
- Uninstall/remove the app.

After that, the stale K badge will disappear.
