Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

What GCC collections and risk leaders ask before an executive briefing.

What is KAIROS?
KAIROS is a GCC-native collections decision intelligence platform. It scores every delinquent account in real time and recommends the next-best action — channel, timing, tone, and treatment — so institutions recover more, earlier in the DPD cycle, at a lower cost to collect.
How long does a proof-of-concept take?
A typical proof-of-concept runs on a single portfolio segment and starts with secure file-based imports, so no core-banking integration is required on day one. Most institutions see the first decision outputs within weeks, with deeper API and core-system integration scheduled once the pilot has proven value.
Does KAIROS support Islamic banking portfolios?
Yes. KAIROS supports profit-rate-based products and Shariah-conscious treatment flows, with bilingual Arabic and English communication templates across SMS, WhatsApp, email, and agent scripts.
Where is our data hosted and how is compliance handled?
KAIROS supports in-country and in-region deployment models for data residency, with encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access control, and a full audit trail on every decision. Treatment strategies are built around CBUAE consumer-protection expectations and each institution's own policy rules.
How does KAIROS integrate with our existing systems?
Integration is staged. Pilots run on secure file transfer (CSV over SFTP); production adds REST APIs and connectors to core banking, loan management, and dialer systems. Outbound channels — SMS, WhatsApp, and email — connect through your approved gateways.
How is KAIROS priced?
Pricing combines a one-time pilot setup fee, a monthly platform fee, and an outcome-linked component tied to incremental recoveries, with annual production tiers based on portfolio size. Request an executive briefing for a model calibrated to your portfolio.
